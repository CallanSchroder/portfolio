This project of mine was created with the intention to mimic the game galactica. I tried to spawn random 'enemies' at random locations accross the map and get them to move downward toward the bottom edge of the screen. I also focused on trying to get the 'defending' side to try and shoot to destroy the oncoming attack. I wasn't able to get the shooting function to work yet however if enemies did come close enough to the spawn point of the 'bullet' they would disappear. Over all very happy with how it came out. Just a bit disappointed that the shooting function didn't come out as intended.
